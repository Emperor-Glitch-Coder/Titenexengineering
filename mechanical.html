High Speed Steel Annular Cutters

Our High Speed Steel (HSS) Broach Cutters are engineered for precision and longevity in standard structural steel applications. Made from premium quality M2 and M35 Cobalt alloy steel, these cutters offer outstanding toughness and excellent chip clearance.

We supply both standard Weldon shank and universal shank HSS cutters with diameters ranging from 12mm to 100mm. Perfect for heavy steel fabrication workshops, construction sites, and repair teams.

High Performance Geometry

Featuring a unique dual-alternate tooth geometry, Ojasvi HSS cutters reduce vibration and heat build-up during drilling, allowing faster penetrations. They can be resharpened multiple times to extend their working lifespan.

  • High Speed Drilling
    The dual-alternate tooth design ensures faster drilling speed and smooth cutting finish with minimal burrs.
  • Superior HSS M35 Cobalt
    For tougher metals, our M35 Cobalt-alloyed HSS cutters offer superior red-hardness and resistance to heat and wear.
  • Resharpening Capability
    Can be easily resharpened on standard annular cutter grinding machines to deliver optimal value.
  • Usage Tips
  • Specifications
  • Applications

Do not apply excessive pressure. Let the cutter do the work. Regularly check coolant levels and clear swarf during deep holes.

Cutting Depth: 25mm, 50mm, 75mm
Diameter Range: 12mm to 100mm
Material: HSS M2 / HSS M35 Cobalt
Shank: 19.05mm Weldon

Designed for general fabrication, structural steel frames, construction beams, and alloy metal plates.
