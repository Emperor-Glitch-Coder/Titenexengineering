Welcome to Ojasvi Group

Ojasvi Group is one of the leading organizations engaged in Manufacturing, export and supply of wide ranges of Magnetic Drilling machines from capacity 35mm dia upto 200mm dia, TCT Broach Cutters from 12mm dia upto 200mm dia, HSS Broach Cutters, TCT cutters for Hard Materials like stainless steel, Hardox etc, Rail Track Drilling Machines in petrol and electrical operated, Carbide Rotary Burrs, Weldon shank twist drills, hole saws and other metal working tools.
