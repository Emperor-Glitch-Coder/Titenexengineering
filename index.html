Our Product Categories

Manufacturer of wide range of Metal Working Tools for customers across India and worldwide

TCT Broach Cutters

TCT Annular Cutters from 12mm to 200mm dia. Available in Standard, Premium and Ultra Premium ranges for all magnetic drills.

HSS Broach Cutters

HSS Annular Cutters with Weldon Shank, 25mm to 100mm cutting depth. Ultra Premium range for hard materials like stainless steel and Hardox.

Magnetic Drills

Wide variety of Magnetic Drills from 30mm to 200mm dia capacity. Available in various models as per industry requirements.

Carbide Rotary Burrs

Wide range of Carbide Rotary Burrs in Single Cut, Double Cut, Aluma Cut, Diamond Cut and special purpose for grinding applications.

Rail Track Drilling Machines

Light weight Rail Track Drilling Machines in petrol and electric variant as per railway standards. Special RAIL track drilling cutters available.

TCT Hole Saw & Twist Drills

TCT Hole Saw, Weldon Shank Twist Drills and Countersink Drills for precision drilling in steel, aluminum and other metals.

We provide innovative Metal Working Solutions for Industrial needs. Our engineering team works to increase productivity and cost effectiveness for our clients.
