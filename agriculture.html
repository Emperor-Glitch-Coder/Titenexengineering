Precision Metal Hole Making Tools

Our TCT Hole Saws, Weldon Shank Twist Drills, and Countersinks are manufactured to meet the needs of installers, fabricators, and contractors. They are ideal for quick, clean hole production in sheet metal, steel panels, aluminum, and plastic sheets.

Ojasvi TCT Hole Saws feature ultra-hard carbide teeth with custom cutting angles, offering significantly faster drilling and longer lifespan compared to standard Bi-Metal hole saws.

Heavy Duty Weldon Shank Drills

Our Weldon shank twist drills allow users to perform standard twist drilling using magnetic drills without needing separate chuck adaptors, reducing runout and saving setup time.

  • TCT Carbide Tips
    Tungsten Carbide tipped teeth outlast standard bi-metal hole saws in stainless steel, and cut up to 10x faster.
  • Ejector Spring & Pilot Drill
    Comes complete with center pilot drill and ejector spring to quickly eject the cut steel core after completion.
  • Weldon Shank Twist Drills
    Drill small holes (6mm to 13mm) directly in your magnetic drill press without an extra adapter.
  • Usage Tips
  • Specifications
  • Applications

Use on hand-held drills with side handles. Do not force or tilt the drill while cutting. Wear eye and hand protection.

Hole Saw Diameter Range: 14mm to 150mm
Weldon Shank Twist Drills: 6mm to 14mm
Cutting Depth: 25mm
Material: Tungsten Carbide tipped

Electrical panels, pipe installations, junction boxes, sheet metal works, HVAC ducting, and light steel fabrication.
